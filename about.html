About Evan

Evan is a fourth-grader and has been in Den 9 of Wannamassa Pack 143 ("The Fighting Ninth") for just over a year. He and his Den are starting their "Webelos" year, where they will learn valuable lessons such as how to be a model citizen and good sport. They will also learn about the outdoors as they participate in several weekend camping trips during the year.

When not fighting against wickedness and oppression throughout the galaxy, Evan enjoys playing kickball, gaga, and Pirate 101
