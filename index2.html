EPISODE IV
A NEW MARKETING CAMPAIGN

It is a time of great sadness in the scouting popcorn community.

 

Evan, the young padawan and Webelos, has been vainly attempting to sell popcorn for his scout troop. He began his campaign as the so-called "King of Popcorn"

 

His father, the Jedi Knight John, developed a web site based on the idea of popcorn as a guilty pleasure taken to the extreme. He even went so far as to associate Evan with seedy, underworld characters such as "Scarface" and Al Capone.

 

While the web site gathered rave reviews, it led to approximately $0 in sales.

 

Seriously. Not one bag of popcorn moved. We didn't even get donations for the military.

 

Eventually, John came to the conclusion that vaguely threatening people in order to get them to buy popcorn was - perhaps - a bad idea. Who can tell these days.

 

Then, Evan's mom said "maybe something with 'Star Wars' in it?"

 

John was dumbfounded at the simple perfection of the idea. He liked Star Wars. People in general like Star Wars.

 

Well, maybe not Episode I.

 

Or Episode II.

 

Or most of Episode III.

 

But the concept is always great. People overcoming adversity to right a great wrong, defeat a great evil, or sell some popcorn.

 

So, please, head on over to Trail's End and buy some popcorn before Disney's lawyers get wind of this.

 

I'm not kidding. One time, they made a small daycare center paint over a picture of Mickey Mouse due to copyright infringement. I can only imagine what they'll do to me...
